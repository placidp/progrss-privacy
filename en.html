Privacy Policy

Progrss App for iOS

Key point: Progrss does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers. All information stays only on your device.

What Data the App Stores

Progrss stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Client names and training information
  • Training schedules
  • Subscription and payment information
  • App settings (theme preferences, etc.)

Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). Data never leaves your phone and is not transmitted to any servers.

Data Collection

The Progrss app:

  • Does not collect usage analytics
  • Does not use advertising trackers
  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Does not require registration or account creation
  • Does not request access to camera, photos, location, or contacts

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through iOS settings: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Progrss → Delete App.

Policy Changes

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the date below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@progrss.app
